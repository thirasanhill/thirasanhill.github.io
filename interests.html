  • Kayaking

    • We have a few kayaks and since our back garden backs onto a tidal waterway which leads to the ocean, we can launch our kayaks from the end of the garden and paddle to the sea.

  • Walking

    • One of my favourite places to go is Khao Chamao National Park which is about 30 minutes drive from our house. I've been going there since I was a little boy, taking the nature trail which goes up the hillside alongside the river, and swimming in the cool water of the river amongst hundreds of fish. The fish are not scared of humans and so they swim close to you when you are in the river.

    I also like to go walking along the beach in the area where I live, and along the nature trails through the mangroves.

  • Caving

    • Thailand has many caves, including some within about 45 minutes drive from our home. It's safe to visit them during dry season. Some caves you can walk into and others you go in by boat along the river which made the cave.

    There's always bats in the caves in Thailand, as well as stalactites and stalagmites (stalactites come down from the ceiling and stalagmites come up from the ground).
