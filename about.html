Where I live

I live with my mum, dad, younger sister and brother in a gated community estate by the ocean called Rock Garden Beach which is near Klaeng in the Rayong province of Thailand.

We have the use of a 50 meter swimming pool which is located right next to the beach and ocean. This is where I learned to swim and was swimming without arm bands from the age of 3.

Our back garden backs onto a tidal waterway which leads to the ocean, so we can launch our kayaks from the end of the garden and paddle to the sea.


Favourite Places

Khao Chamao
One of my favourite places to go is Khao Chamao National Park which is about 30 minutes drive from our house. I've been going there since I was a little boy, taking the nature trail which goes up the hillside alongside the river, and swimming in the cool water of the river amongst hundreds of fish. The fish are not scared of humans and so they swim close to you when you are in the river.
Gym Workout
We have our own gym at one of the houses on the estate and so me and my dad go weight training together. I started weight training when I was 10 years old and this year I am growing quickly and I am now taller than my mum.
